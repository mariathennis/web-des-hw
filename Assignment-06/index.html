Peanut Butter Cookies

Ingredients

  • 1 cup smooth peanut butter
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
    •

    Directions

    1. Preheat oven to 350oF.
    2. In a medium bowl, combine peanut butter, sugar, eggs and baking soda.
    3. Roll into small sized balls and create a criss cross pattern with a fork.
    4. Bake for 10-12 minutes until lightly brown, and let cool.
