Peanut Butter Cookie Sandwiches

Ingredients

Step 1- Gather Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup all purpose flower
  • 1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/3 cup butter
  • 3/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup creamy peanut butter
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract



Step 2- Make the cookies

  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees and line two large baking sheets with parchment paper
  • Whisk the flour, oats, baking soda, baking powder, and salt until combined
  • In a large bowl using a hand-held mixer beat the butter, brown sugar, and granulated sugar together until smooth and creamy. Add the peanut butter, egg and vanilla extract.
  • scoop cookie dough into two balls, gently press down on balls to flaten
  • bake for 10-11 minutes
  • Make the frosting: In a large bowl beat 1 cup creamy peanut butter, 1 tablespoon honey, 1/4 cup confectioners sugar
  • pipe onto flat side of one cookie and place other cookie ontop
